# Coach prompts (templates)

- checkin: "Hi {name}, how was your workout today? Any soreness or issues?"
- appointment_reminder: "Reminder: You have a session on {date} at {time}. Reply CONFIRM to keep it."
- progress_summary: "Weekly summary: you trained {sessions} sessions, average intensity {intensity}. Keep it up!"

Use these templates as starting points for scheduler-driven messages.
