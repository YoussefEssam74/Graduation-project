Frequently Asked Questions

View all
How do I freeze my membership? expand_more
You can freeze your membership directly in the app under Account Settings > Membership > Freeze. You can freeze for a minimum of 1 week and a maximum of 3 months per year.
Does the AI Coach sync with Apple Health? expand_more
Yes! PulseGym integrates seamlessly with Apple Health. Go to App Settings > Integrations and toggle "Apple Health Sync" to ON.
Can I bring a guest? expand_more
Premium members get 2 guest passes per month. Standard members can purchase a day pass for a guest at the front desk or via the app for $15.
What happens if I miss a booked class? expand_more
If you do not cancel at least 2 hours before the class starts, a "No Show" fee may be applied, or you may lose your class credit.
